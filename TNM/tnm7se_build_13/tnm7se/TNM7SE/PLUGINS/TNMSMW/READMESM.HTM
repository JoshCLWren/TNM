Main Form
1) Select the wrestler you want in the treeview control on the left.
2) If the wrestler has already been assigned special moves, they will appear on the top right hand side.
3a) To edit a move already assigned, double-click the appropriate line in the top right hand window.
3b) To add a new move, click "New Move".
3c) To delete a new move, click the appropriate line and hit the [DEL] key.
4) In cases 3a and 3b, a new form will pop up.
5) This form has three fields:
  • Generic Move Name: Click the arrow on the right to bring up a list of all moves in your TNM 7 SE.
  • XXX's Moveset: This list contains all regular moves that particular wrestler performs. Double-click any entry in the listbox to select it for the Generic Move Name.
  • Special Move Name: This is where you enter the name the move has for this particular wrestler. Please remember to supply the proper article, i.e. "a", "an" or "the".

    You can also rename an entire class of moves. This makes sense for wrestlers like Tazz who performs Tazzplexes instead of suplexes or Rick Steiner who does Steinerlines instead of regular clotheslines. You do this by beginning the "Generic Move Name" field with an asterisk (*):

    What this means is: "replace the part 'Suplex' with 'Tazplex' in all moves". These names are case-sensitive, i.e. you should have an entry for '*suplex' and an entry for '*Suplex'. Please remember: the asterisk always goes first. Do not use more than one asterisk per entry.

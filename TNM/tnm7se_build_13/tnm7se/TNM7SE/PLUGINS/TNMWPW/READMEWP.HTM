Main Form
The main form is divided up into two sections.

Section 1: Wrestler List

   Use this section to determine, whom you want to assign working-the-crowd strings.

Situation 1: The wrestler is in the listbox already
This means that crowd chants for the wrestler have already been assigned. Just click his name in the list to bring up his working-the-crowd strings.

Situation 2: The wrestler isn't in the listbox yet but is in the drop-down list on top of the frame
This means that the wrestler doesn't yet work the crowd, however he is in TNM's wrestler database. Click his name in the drop-down list (1, 2) and then click "Add Wrestler" (3) to add him to the list (4).

Situation 3: The wrestler isn't in the listbox yet nor is he in the drop-down list on top of the frame
This means the wrestler doesn't exist in TNM's wrestler database, nor are there any pre-assigned working-the-crowd strings for him. Enter his name into the drop-down box (1), then click "Add Wrestler" (2) to bring him into the list (3).

Section 2: Wrestler Interaction With The Crowd

  There are three different categories in which you can assign a wrestler to work the crowd:
  1. Work As Face: when the wrestler is working as a face, he will work the crowd using these strings
  2. Work As Heel: when the wrestler is working as a heel, he will work the crowd using these strings
  3. Work As Either: these are used regardless of a wrestler's face or heel status - e.g. Ric Flair's 'WHOOO'.
Imporant: All three types can be assigned at the same time.

To assign, edit or delete something, just click the relevant tab (Face, Heel, Either) and then edit the text in the text box below - one working-the-crowd string per line.

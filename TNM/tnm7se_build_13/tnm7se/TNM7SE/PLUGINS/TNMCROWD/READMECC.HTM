Main Form
The main form is divided up into two sections.

Section 1: Wrestler List

   Use this section to determine, whose crowd chants you'd like to edit.

Situation 1: The wrestler is in the listbox already
This means that crowd chants for the wrestler have already been assigned. Just click his name in the list to bring up his chants.

Situation 2: The wrestler isn't in the listbox yet but is in the drop-down list on top of the frame
This means that no chants are available for this wrestler yet, however he is in TNM's wrestler database. Click his name in the drop-down list (1, 2) and then click "Add Wrestler" (3) to add him to the list (4).

Situation 3: The wrestler isn't in the listbox yet nor is he in the drop-down list on top of the frame
This means the wrestler doesn't exist in TNM's wrestler database, nor are there any pre-assigned chants for him. Enter his name into the drop-down box (1), then click "Add Wrestler" (2) to bring him into the list (3).

Section 2: The Crowd Chants

  There are three different chant categories:
  1. Face Chants: the crowd chants these when a wrestler is a face to them
  2. Heel Chants: the crowd chants these when a wrestler is a heel to them
  3. Status-Independent Chants: these are used regardless of a wrestler's face or heel status - e.g. Ric Flair's 'WHOOO'.
Imporant: Face and Heel chants can be assigned at the same time. However, if you assign Status-Independent Chants, the Face and Heel chant tabs should remain empty.

To assign, edit or delete chants, just click the relevant tab (Face, Heel, Status-Independent) and then edit the text in the text box below - one chant per line.
